How Do

The idea for this project came when we heard about the inbalance of genders in S.T.E.M roles.

Inspired by old childrens books that explained how things in the world worked, How Do is an attempt to build up knowledge that everyone can access. And hopefully at the same time break down the gender barriers.

We thought if we could make S.T.E.M just that little bit more accessible, we might encourage people to consider a career in one of the fields.

A more secret goal for doing this is to make a small dent in climate change. An increased and more diverse work force can only be positive. People working in S.T.E.M will be so important in our fight against the damage we are doing to this planet.
