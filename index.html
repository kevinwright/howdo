HowDo is built on a simple idea.

Children are often told or feel that they can't do things because of their gender or intelligence. The things we’re told and learn as children leave a mark. If a child is told by a teacher that they are lazy, they will carry that ‘tag’ with them for years.

Science, technology, engineering and maths make up the STEM subjects. They are some of the most important and interesting subjects that children will learn. But they can be unaccessible - hard to understand and weighted towards boys.

HowDo explains these subjects to children in a way that is accessible and easy to understand. Whatever their gender or ability, we hope children will be engaged and fascinated by how things in our world work.
