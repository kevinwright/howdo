5 ways to save the planet

That benefit you. So you don't have to worry about the planet.

Cycle and walk

#1

Ever looked around at cars on the road on your way to work and noticed how 90% only have one person. Imagine replacing all those one person car journeys with bikes or pedestrians.

This is easy. Cycle or walk instead of driving. You don't even have to do it all the time.

The benefits are staggering. Less traffic. Healthier people - which leads to less strain on our health services. Saving money.

Recycle... more

#2

Most of us recycle. There are so many things to recycle. Food that gets turned back into energy. Metal that gets turned into something (helpful).

But we can all do more. Don't throw yogurt cups. Wash them. You can also recycle the coffee grounds from pods if you use them.

So many positives. And it makes way lighter bin bags to carry up the drive.

Weekday vegetarian

#5

Meat production is doing the most damage to our planet.

Becoming a full on vegetarian isn't for everyone. But it's easy to avoid meat in the week and keep it as a weekend treat. It's healthier, saves you money and helps you stay trim.
